SPOKE Local Audio Player

Play audio files directly from your computer

Setup

How it works:
  1. Click "Select Audio Folder" and choose a folder on your PC
  2. The browser gets permission to read files from that folder
  3. Audio files appear below and can be played instantly
  4. Assign files to SPOKE pads for MIDI triggering
  5. No uploading needed - files stay on your computer!
