How SPOKE maps to the circle of fifths
SPOKE's 27 pads sit in a circle, spanning C2 → D7 chromatically — one semitone per step downward.
Select a key on the circle of fifths and the board highlights every pad whose MIDI note belongs to that major scale. Play highlighted pads to stay in key.
Adjacent pads differ by 1 semitone. Moving 7 pads = a perfect fifth. Moving 5 pads = a perfect fourth.
Because the board is fully chromatic, pad interval = semitone count: a major triad is root + 4 pads + 7 pads.
